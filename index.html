About the Congress關於大會

“Beyond the Algorithm: Elevating Construction Cost Management through Big Data, Digital Twins, and Cognitive AI” “超越演算法：透過大數據, 數字孿生和認知人工智能提升建築成本管理”

The ICEC-PAQS Joint World Congress 2028 brings together the International Cost Engineering Council and the Pacific Association of Quantity Surveyors to explore how next‑generation technologies are transforming cost engineering, project controls, and quantity surveying across the Asia‑Pacific region and beyond. ICEC-PAQS 2028 聯合世界大會匯聚了國際成本工程委員會 與太平洋工料測量師協會, 共同探討下一代科技如何改變亞太地區及全球的 成本工程、項目控制和工料測量領域。

Host Organisation主辦機構

The Hong Kong Institute of Surveyors 香港測量師學會

The Hong Kong Institute of Surveyors is the professional body representing the surveying profession in Hong Kong. We are proud to host this premier event, fostering innovation and collaboration in the built environment. 香港測量師學會是代表香港測量專業的專業團體。 我們很榮幸能夠主辦這次盛會, 促進建築環境領域的創新與合作。
